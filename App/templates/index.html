Are you a scuba diver who wants to explore species outside of your local water-scene?

Do you seek to travel to other sea beds but don’t know what marine life you’ll be encountering?

Are you forced to travel to a landmass away from home and are desperate to tour new underwater vistas and its inhabitants?

Let Marine Buddy be your guide!

Marine Buddy is here to help you find your next diving adventure in a marine-conscious way. You can use our Country Search to surf through different species and review a graphical representation of their status, or deep-dive into each species directly on our Marine Life Search page, which links to their IUCN Red List profile.

To learn more about our methodology, check out the How Do We Do It page!
